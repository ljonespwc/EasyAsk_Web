Rethinking web analytics: A founder's perspective

Hi, digital marketing team!

The problem Goalzilla solves for web teams is the huge disconnect between data and knowing how to best use it.

Traditional analytics tools provide a wealth of datapageviews, bounce rates, time on site, click-through ratesbut they fail to provide the context needed to truly understand visitor behavior and motivation. This leaves marketing teams with a lot of "what" information, but very little "why" insight.

These tools track actions without capturing intentions. They show you that someone visited a page, clicked a button, or left the site, but they can't tell you what the person was trying to accomplish. This missing piecevisitor intentis crucial for making informed decisions about website design and navigation, content strategy, and overall user experience.

Further, traditional tools treat all visitors the same, failing to recognize that different people have different goals. This one-size-fits-all approach can lead to misinterpretations of data and suboptimal marketing strategies.

Goalzilla bridges this gap by connecting visitor intent with behavior and outcomes. It allows marketers, copywriters, and designers to see the full journeyfrom what brings someone to the site, to what they're trying to accomplish, to how well the site meets those needs. This holistic view enables you to:

  1. Understand why certain pages are more successful than others
  2. Identify where visitors with specific intents are struggling
  3. Tailor content and user paths to better meet visitor goals
  4. Measure success not just in terms of actions, but in terms of satisfied user intentions

Goalzilla solves the problem of incomplete understanding. It transforms raw data into actionable insights by providing the crucial context of user intent, allowing you to make more informed, user-centric decisions across all aspects of your digital strategy.

It's web analytics you can get excited about!

Lance Jones, Goalzilla Co-founder
